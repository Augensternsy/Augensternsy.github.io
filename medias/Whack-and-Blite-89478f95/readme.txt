Whack and Blite
===============

Author: Bouzounours

Notes:
These were made for my own use, I just wanted something a bit chunkier to aim my mouse with!

PS : the "waiting" cursors are animated.

PPS : DA didn't like me trying to upload a zip file so for any of you having trouble with the tar format there's a zip version of the cursors here -> [link]

Download the zip file and extract the contents somewhere easy to find on your hard drive.

Assuming you have Windows, click on Start- Settings-Control Panel and double click the Mouse icon. Click on the Pointers tab. Select the particular cursor you want to replace and click on Browse. Navigate to the location on your hard drive where you saved the cursors, click on the particular one you'd like to use and click on the Open button.

You'll see the new cursor in the preview box. If it's the one you want to keep, click Apply and move on to the next cursor you want to replace.

This animated cursor set contains 15 cursors.

Files included:
  - resize diagonal 2.cur
  - resize horizontal.cur
  - resize vertical.cur
  - pen.cur
  - resize diagonal 1.cur
  - link.cur
  - unavailable.cur
  - text.cur
  - precise.cur
  - move.cur
  - help.cur
  - normal.cur
  - alternate.cur
  - background.ani
  - busy.ani

Installation:
  1. Right-click install.inf
  2. Select 'Install'
  3. Open Control Panel > Mouse > Pointers
  4. Select 'Whack and Blite' from the Scheme dropdown
